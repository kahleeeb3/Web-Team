Welcome to the Computer Science Club

What is CS Club?

The Wabash College CS Club is a group of Wabash Students formed in Fall of 2021. Members include CS majors, and minors, as well as people with a general interest in CS. In addition to being a registered club in the Student Senate, we are also a registered Student Chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery. Officially, our title is "Wabash College ACM Student Chapter", however, our friends just call us "CS Club".

What does CS do?

  1. Weekly Meeting with different activities
  2. Fun discussion group for friends
  3. Programming related challenges
  4. Guest Speakers
  5. Whatever people want! Let us know.
